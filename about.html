Hello, I am CorDarryl E. Hall

Biography

At Marriott International, CorDarryl Hall holds the position of Senior Manager for Compensation Data Management and Reporting. As part of my role, I am responsible for various tasks including developing BI Publisher reports using SQL for accurate and efficient data modeling and reporting in Oracle HCM transactional reporting. Additionally, I manage partner vendors to address any issues or enhancements and keep track of vendor activities while overseeing a small team of three analysts responsible for optimizing crucial compensation data management processes and reports necessary for business operations.

I also design and enhance processes to manage compensation data such as bonus plans and compensation zones while ensuring that business requirements are met through interaction with subject matter experts from various clients. I am skilled in presenting ideas to clients and developing effective compensation reporting solutions within agreed-upon timelines. As a project leader, I manage resources and timelines to achieve desired outcomes in a team environment, successfully handling multiple projects and processes within budget constraints. Building relationships with internal and external stakeholders across regions, brands, and disciplines is another key aspect of my role.

Before serving in this role, CorDarryl was a Senior Business Analyst with Intel Corporation and was responsible for leading a team of 11 analysts to consult on business intelligence throughout the Sales & Marketing organization. He also was responsible for helping his team drive engineering objectives of introducing over $6 million in computer products that caused a record breaking achievement in over a 10 year period.

Prior to joining Intel, CorDarryl served in the United States Navy on board a submarine as a Machinist Mate and later converted to a Logistics Specialist. Serving in the U.S Navy, he was responsible for leading a team of 6 that controlled over $20 million worth of equipment while serving the Pacific West on 3 separate deployments.

Earning an honorable discharge, he decided to earn his Masters of Business Administration (MBA) from Willamette University in 2019. While earning his MBA, he consulted with the Boys & Girls club of West Salem, OR by saving them over $500K and helped improve several business processes.

CorDarryl spends his free time with his two dogs, Roman and Ramona.  He is the 3rd of 8 siblings who were raised in Chicago, IL.  He did his undergraduate program in sunny Arizona before joining the military stationed in Hawaii and Washington State.  He enjoys chess, architecture, photography, bowling, and the adrenaline rush of skydiving.  He's also a self-proclaimed movie buff.
